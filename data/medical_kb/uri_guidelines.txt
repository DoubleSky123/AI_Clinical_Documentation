Upper Respiratory Infection (URI) / Common Cold — Clinical Guidelines
======================================================================

DIAGNOSIS
---------
URI is a clinical diagnosis. No routine testing needed. Characterized by:
- Nasal congestion and rhinorrhea (hallmark symptoms)
- Sore throat (often first symptom)
- Cough (may persist 2–3 weeks)
- Low-grade fever or no fever
- Mild malaise

Average duration: 7–10 days. Cough may persist up to 3 weeks.

Common pathogens: Rhinovirus (most common), coronavirus, adenovirus,
RSV (infants/elderly), parainfluenza.

ICD-10 CODES
------------
J00    — Acute nasopharyngitis (common cold)
J06.9  — Acute upper respiratory infection, unspecified
J02.9  — Acute pharyngitis, unspecified
J03.90 — Acute tonsillitis, unspecified

DISTINGUISHING URI FROM BACTERIAL INFECTIONS
---------------------------------------------
Bacterial sinusitis suggested by: symptoms >10 days without improvement,
severe localized sinus pain/pressure, fever >39°C, double worsening
(improves then worsens).

Strep pharyngitis (GAS): Centor criteria — tonsillar exudates, tender anterior
cervical nodes, fever, absence of cough. Score >= 3 → rapid strep test indicated.

TREATMENT — ANTIBIOTICS NOT INDICATED FOR VIRAL URI
-----------------------------------------------------
Symptomatic relief only:

Nasal congestion:
  - Intranasal saline irrigation
  - Pseudoephedrine 30–60mg q4–6h PRN (avoid in HTN)
  - Oxymetazoline nasal spray x 3 days max (rebound congestion risk)

Sore throat / fever:
  - Acetaminophen 500–1000mg q4–6h PRN
  - Ibuprofen 400–600mg q6–8h PRN with food
  - Throat lozenges / saltwater gargle

Cough:
  - Honey (adults and children >1 year) — evidence-based
  - Dextromethorphan 15–30mg q4–6h PRN
  - Guaifenesin 200–400mg q4h PRN (expectorant)

RETURN PRECAUTIONS
------------------
- Symptoms not improving after 10 days
- Fever >39°C or returning after initial resolution
- Severe facial pain/pressure (possible sinusitis)
- Ear pain (possible otitis media)
- Difficulty breathing or swallowing
